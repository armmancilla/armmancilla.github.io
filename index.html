EXPERIENCE

Parsimonious Epidemic Models for Heard Immunity and Social Distancing
PRO-SNI Scholarship. May 2021 to September 2021
Working under the supervision of Dr. Thomas Gorin, we implemented an epidemic model with exponential growth and variable infection rate to infected waves during the COVID-19 pandemic.
  • Processed and cleaned CONACYT-provided data using Pandas, documenting methodology in Jupyter Notebook.
  • Identified distinct infection waves and fitted an exponential-growth model to COVID-19 case data in Mexico.
  • Derived analytical solutions where possible and linked findings to the Susceptible-Infected-Removed (SIR) model.
  • Calculated key parameters to model infection rate dynamics at the start and end of each wave.
